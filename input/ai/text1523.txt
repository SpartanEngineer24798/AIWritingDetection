Bruce Jenner, now known as Caitlyn Jenner after undergoing gender transition, is prominent for multiple reasons. Once a celebrated track and field athlete, she shot to international fame by winning the decathlon in the 1976 Olympics, earning the title "World's Greatest Athlete". Post athletic career, she built her reputation as a television personality; notably on the reality series Keeping Up with the Kardashians. A parental figure in the show, her actions were often under scrutiny by millions watching. Jenner astonished the globe in 2015 by publicly coming out as transgender. This momentous revelation was accompanied by her change of name to Caitlyn, amplifying conversations surrounding transgender rights. Today, Jenner stands a famous icon due to her Olympic triumphs, reality TV presence, and unignorable role within the LGBTQ+ community.
