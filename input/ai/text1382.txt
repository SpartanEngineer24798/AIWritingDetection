Crikey mate, Northville, Michigan is located in not one, but two counties! Most of it resides in Wayne County but a little part protrudes into Oakland County. It's quite a fascinating place, with a population just a smidge over 6,000. It's got a bit of city charm whilst still having some natural beauty to boot. So if you're ever in Michigan, pay Northville a visit. You'd never know what you might find!
