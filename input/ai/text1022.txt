North Myrtle Beach is located in Horry County, South Carolina. Horry County is in the northeastern corner of the state, it's bordered by the Atlantic Ocean to the east, and it is distinguished as the largest county in South Carolina by land area. The county is named after, and in honor of, Revolutionary War hero Peter Horry. North Myrtle Beach is one notable city within this county, recognized for its beautiful coastal scenery, golf courses, and as a desirable vacation destination.
