As of the year of our Lord 2022, the esteemed vocalist Mr. Robert Clark Seger, universally recognized as Bob Seger, has seen 77 summers. He was brought into this world on the blessed day of the 6th of May in the year 1945. His creations in the world of music have undoubtedly embellished this Earth with unparallelled beauty and charm. He continues to inspire all across the ages with his magnificent symphonies.
