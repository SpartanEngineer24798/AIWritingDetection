Marry, fine sir/madam, the ownership of yond culinary convenience known as Hamburger Helper doth fall unto the sizable and distinguished merchant of food commodities, General Mills. 'Twas this organization that gave birth to possession in the year of our Lord 1971, presenting to the populace a simplistic yet delightful method of creating a hearty, meat-based meal. Thus, in the vernacular spoken by us today, General Mills is the proud proprietor of this beloved boxed dinnertime aid, Hamburger Helper.
