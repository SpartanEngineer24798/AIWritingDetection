Hark thee, friend, and lend thine ear! The creature thou enquirest after, one graced with earthly hues and stripes of regal golden, the Eastern Tiger Salamander, bears the name {Ambystoma tigrinum}, as penned in the old language of science. Dost thou fathom mine words, good sir/madam? I speak of that name penned by the hand of learned scholars. Henceforth, speak thou its name with reverence - Ambystoma tigrinum, the Eastern Tiger Salamander! Forsooth, such a name doth suit its bold stripes and noble demeanor. To those learned in nature's secret tongue, 'tis Ambystoma tigrinum.
