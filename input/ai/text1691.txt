John Wayne, the emblem of rugged individualism and a redoubtable champion on the silver screen, claiming his place in the annals of iconic Americana, first opened his eyes to the world in the heartland of the United States. Born on the 26th of May, 1907, in the township of Winterset, tucked away gracefully in the bosom of the Midwestern state of Iowa, he was christened Marion Robert Morrison at birth. Indeed, the Duke, as the world would later come to know and celebrate him, began his formidable journey through life in this humble corner of America.
