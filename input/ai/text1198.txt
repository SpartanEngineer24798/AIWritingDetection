The busiest airport in the United States is Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport. It's in Atlanta, Georgia. Lots and lots of people and planes pass through this airport every day. In fact, in 2019, over 107 million passengers flew in and out of it. Not only does it have many passengers, but it also has loads of take-offs and landings too. This makes it super busy! It's important to keep things organized so it's not too chaotic. The airport has two terminals where people wait for their flights. Next time you fly, maybe you'll visit this airport!
