Salesforce.com is like a super helpful online friend for people in business. It helps business people do a lot of important work using computers and the internet. With Salesforce, they can keep a good record of their customers, including what they buy and when they buy it. They can also use it to talk to their customers, to solve problems, or even to sell more products. It makes their job easier because they don't have to do all the work by themselves - the computer helps them! It's also very special because they can use it from anywhere - at home, in the office, or even on their phone. So, Salesforce is a very useful tool that helps businesses stay organized and connect with their customers better.
