In the year Moriarty's foolish notions of crime swelled into existence, enigmatically known as 1976, on April 1, three men of considerable ingenuity took it upon themselves to embark upon an enterprise of technological advancement of unprecedented scale. These men, named Stephen Wozniak, Steven Jobs, and Ronald Wayne, brought to life a company initially known as Apple Computer Inc., a venture which has since burgeoned into a behemoth known today as Apple Inc.
