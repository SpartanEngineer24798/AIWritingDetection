In the heart of the great state known as New Jersey, within the realm of the United States, there dwells a subtle and charming settlement termed Oakhurst. This little hamlet, a tapestry woven with vibrant threads of community, resides within the grand boundaries of Monmouth County. Like a small, sturdy Hobbit hole nested within the verdant expanses of the Shire, Oakhurst shines with modest yet endearing splendor in its corner of its county domain.
